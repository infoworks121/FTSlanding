

# FTS Landing Website Plan

## Overview
A premium, modern landing page for the FTS (Farm & Tech Service) ecosystem with futuristic design, glassmorphism UI, green color palette (matching the logo), smooth animations, and professional corporate look.

## Design System
- **Primary colors**: Green gradient palette (from the FTS logo - deep green to lime green)
- **UI style**: Glassmorphism cards, subtle gradients, clean typography
- **Animations**: Framer Motion for scroll reveals, hover effects, and hero animations
- **Logo**: Embedded FTS logo from uploaded file

## Sections to Build

### 1. Navigation Bar
- Sticky header with glass effect, FTS logo, section links, and "Join Network" CTA button

### 2. Hero Section
- Headline: "Revolutionizing Business Ecosystem with FTS"
- Subheading about smart automated multi-layer business network
- Two CTA buttons: "Explore System" and "Join Network"
- Animated network/particle background with green theme

### 3. About Company
- What is FTS, Vision, Mission statements
- CEO/CTO placeholder cards with bios

### 4. How FTS Works (System Flow)
- Visual flow diagram: Admin → Core Body → Businessman → Customer
- Animated step-by-step explanation with icons

### 5. Role Detail Sections
- **Admin Panel** – "The Brain" with product onboarding, category management, profit rules, stock distribution
- **Core Body Panel** – District authority, inventory control, Core Body A & B, investment model
- **Businessman Panel** – Unified role, earning methods (margin, bulk, referral, fulfillment), zero investment highlight
- **Customer Panel** – Marketplace, smart fulfillment, order tracking

### 6. Profit Distribution Infographic
- Visual B2B and B2C profit flow with animated charts/graphics

### 7. Features Section
- Automation, scalability, transparency, multi-layer earnings — icon cards with glassmorphism

### 8. Why Choose Us
- Key differentiators with animated counters and benefit cards

### 9. Testimonials & Future Vision
- Testimonial cards with slider, future roadmap timeline

### 10. Contact Section
- Contact form (name, email, phone, message) with glassmorphism card
- Social links and company info

### 11. Footer
- Links, copyright, logo

## Technical Approach
- Install `framer-motion` for animations
- Copy FTS logo to project assets
- Build all sections as separate components
- Fully responsive (mobile-first)
- Smooth scroll navigation between sections
